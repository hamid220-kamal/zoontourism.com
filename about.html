Our Story

Welcome to Zoon Tourism—a travel company born from a deep love for Kashmir and inspired by its timeless beauty.

In Kashmiri, “Zoon” (زوٗن) means moon—a symbol of serenity, radiance, and wonder. Just as the moon softly lights Kashmir’s night skies, we aspire to illuminate your journey through this magical land, guiding you with care, warmth, and authenticity.

At Zoon Tourism, we are not just another travel service. We are storytellers of Kashmir, curating journeys that go beyond sightseeing. From the majestic Himalayas crowned with snow to the mirror-like lakes reflecting the sky, every experience is designed to immerse you in the Valley’s natural splendor and cultural soul.

We believe travel should be personal, meaningful, and responsible. That’s why we work closely with local communities, ensuring that every trip supports livelihoods, preserves traditions, and leaves a positive impact on both travelers and hosts.

Whether you dream of peaceful moments by the banks of the Jhelum, exhilarating adventures across the mountains of Gulmarg, a quiet shikara ride on Dal Lake, or exploring the centuries-old heritage of Srinagar, Zoon Tourism opens doors to experiences that stay with you long after you return home.

Our story is not just about tourism—it is about connection. Connection with people, with culture, and with the land itself. When you travel with Zoon Tourism, you don’t just see Kashmir, you live it.
