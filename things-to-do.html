Things to Do in Kashmir

Unforgettable experiences await you

Best Time for Activities

Kashmir offers different activities throughout the year. Plan your visit based on your interests!

Winter

Dec - Feb
Skiing, Snowboarding

Spring

Mar - May
Gardens, Trekking

Summer

Jun - Aug
Rafting, Camping

Autumn

Sep - Nov
Photography, Hiking

Shikara Ride

Relaxation

Experience the serene beauty of Dal Lake on a traditional wooden shikara. Glide through floating gardens and witness the magical sunrise or sunset over the mountains.

1-2 hours ₹500-800 4.8/5

Skiing & Snowboarding

Adventure

Hit the slopes at Gulmarg, one of Asia's premier ski destinations. Professional instructors and world-class equipment available for all skill levels.

Full Day ₹3000-5000 4.9/5

Mountain Trekking

Adventure

Explore Kashmir's pristine valleys and mountain passes. Trek through alpine meadows, glaciers, and remote villages with experienced guides.

3-7 days ₹5000-15000 4.7/5

River Rafting

Adventure

Navigate the rapids of Lidder River in Pahalgam. Experience thrilling white-water rafting with safety equipment and expert guides.

2-3 hours ₹1500-2500 4.6/5

Gulmarg Gondola

Nature

Ride Asia's highest cable car to 13,000 feet. Witness breathtaking panoramic views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks and valleys.

2-3 hours ₹1500-2000 5.0/5

Horse Riding

Relaxation

Explore the beautiful meadows and valleys of Kashmir on horseback. Perfect for all ages and experience levels with trained guides.

1-4 hours ₹800-2000 4.5/5

Mountain Camping

Adventure

Spend nights under the stars in the beautiful valleys of Kashmir. Bonfire, local cuisine, and breathtaking mountain views included.

1-3 nights ₹2000-5000 4.8/5

Mughal Gardens

Cultural

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens - Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Experience the architectural marvels and beautiful landscapes.

3-4 hours ₹300-500 4.6/5

Photography Tour

Relaxation

Capture the stunning landscapes of Kashmir with professional photography guides. Perfect for amateur and professional photographers alike.

4-6 hours ₹2500-4000 4.9/5

Kashmiri Cuisine

Cultural

Experience authentic Kashmiri cuisine including Wazwan, Rogan Josh, and Kahwa tea. Cooking classes and food tours available.

2-3 hours ₹1000-2500 4.7/5

Houseboat Stay

Relaxation

Stay in traditional Kashmiri houseboats on Dal Lake. Experience luxury accommodation with beautiful lake views and authentic Kashmiri hospitality.

Overnight ₹4000-10000 4.8/5

Golf at Gulmarg

Relaxation

Play golf at one of the highest golf courses in the world. Beautiful mountain backdrop and well-maintained greens await golf enthusiasts.

3-4 hours ₹1500-3000 4.5/5
